About

Junkun Jiang (姜俊锟)

I am now a senior algorithm engineer working for BIGO, a short video provider and a global live video social platform. My job is to develop enhancing algorithms based on image processing and video processing.

I am interested in Computer Vision and Image Processing, especially in sketch-based image retrieval, sketch segmentation, and 3D model segmentation. Recently, I am working on saliency detection and similar object segmentation technologies for video stream bitrate reducing and detail enhancement. Due to the deploy requirement, I also have the ability to accelerate the NN model via TensorRT and OpenVINO, and Neon intrinsic based on mobile deployment tasks. Besides, I have a little bit of understanding of the prune and light-weighted methods.

In my spare time, I enjoy jogging and photography. I love basketball.
